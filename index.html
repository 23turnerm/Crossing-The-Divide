ABOUT US

Our organization aims to enrich political discussion in high schools by exposing students to a variety of perspectives and arguments

political polarization

Political polarization is at an all-time high, and the division has only grown over recent years. We wish to lower this troubling trend by providing a place where one can discuss their ideas with others and understand the other side.

learn more
hearing from different people

We often only hear what we agree with and end up in an echo-chamber where different perspectives are difficult to come across. We aim to create a place where one can exchange and sharpen their ideas while overcoming the existing barrier that pervades political discussion today.

reaching common ground

As we evolve our discourse and learn to articulate our ideas better, we will be able to find common ground with those with whom we disagree with and find areas where we can come together to reach a common goal.

overcoming the divide

Through this club we will overcome the deep-rooted polarization that exists within our society by learning to empathize with different perspectives.

rules

  • keep a courteous tone
  • do not insult your interlocutor, and instead address their arguments
  • participants must follow the instructions of the sitting moderator
  • all opinions should be heard and discussed, and while one is doing so they are entitled to time without interruptions
  • if a specific topic becomes a little too personal for someone, they can raise their hand and ask that we move to the next topic
