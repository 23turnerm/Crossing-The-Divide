political polarization

Political Polarization is at an all-time high, and the division has only grown over recent years. We wish to lower this troubling trend by providing a place where one can discuss their ideas with others and understand the other side.

learn more
hearing from different people

We often only hear what we agree with, and end up in an echo-chamber where different perspectives are difficult to come across. We aim to create a place where one can exchange and sharpen their ideas while overcoming the barrier in political discussion that exists today

reach common ground

Throughout this experience we will see areas of disagreement. However, we also may see and reach a common ground. One big thing that you may find in conversations is that even though there may be different perspectives initially, there are going to be areas in which common ground is reached.

overcoming the divide

through this club we will overcome the vast divide that exists within politics today and learn to empathize with those we disagree with

rules

  • keep a courteous tone
  • do not insult your interlocutor, and instead address their arguments
  • participants must follow the instructions of the sitting moderator
  • All opinions should be heard and discussed, and while one is doing so they are entitled to time without interruptions
  • If a specific topic becomes a little too personal for someone, they can raise their hand and ask that we move to the next topic
